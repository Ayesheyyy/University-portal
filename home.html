About Us

Welcome to Barani Institute of Science

Barani Institute of Sciences was established in 2014, having campuses in Sahiwal and Burewala which are easily accessible by public.

PMAS Arid Agriculture University is located in Rawalpindi almost in the center of the twin cosmopolitan cities of Rawalpindi & Islamabad, the capital of Pakistan. Upon the successful completion of their studies, they are awarded the degrees by PMAS Arid Agriculture University, Rawalpindi.
